SIA N'SON MICROFINANCE
How to Apply

Potential clients should consult with Sia N'Son agents at the nearest Sia N'Son office. Telephone numbers, email addresses and office addresses are available on the contact page. For the most effective meeting, potential borrowers should come with a list of co-borrowers and be prepared to discuss their prospective business plans.

To open an account, each client will need to present himself in person along with:

  • one piece of government issued identification
  • two passport-sized photographs
  • appropriate cash to cover account-opening and administration fees
  • appropriate cash for the security deposit
