Job Opportunities

There are currently no openings for paid positions at Sia N'Son. We encourage you to submit your resume nonetheless for future considerations.

Internship Opportunities

Sia N'Son has a strong summer internship program. Interested university students should contact the Executive Director, M. Amadou, with a cover letter and resume of relevant studies and experience. Internships are available only at the Parakou office, are unpaid and usually last two to three months.
