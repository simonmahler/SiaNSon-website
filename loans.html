Micro-credits

Micro-CrÈdit au Plus Pauvres (MCPP)
Micro-credit for the most poor
MCPP is a program designed for groups of 5-15 first time borrowers with little or no starting capital. The two stages serve to prepare new borrowers for the transition into one of Sia N'Sonís higher-level micro-credit programs. MCPP is funded by Fonds National de la Microfinance. Details of the program are outlined below:*
First time borrower Second time borrower
Maximum Loan Size 30,000 50,000
Annual Interest Rate 2% 3%
Duration (months) 6 6
Payment Frequency monthly monthly
Security Deposit Requirement initially none but generated by additional 3% interest initially none but generated by additional 5% interest
Fees
Liability 500
Registration 300
Administration 200
Liability 500
Registration 300
Administration 200

Tout Petit CrÈdit aux Femmes (TPCF)
Small loans for women
TPCF is a program designed for large initiatives of 10 to 60 people with medium credit needs. Groups do not have to be composed of women only. It is common for groups to take consecutive TPCF loans provided they have a good repayment history. TPCF loans are mostly funded by the United Nations Development Program (PNUD). Details of the program are outlined below:*
All borrowers
Maximum Loan Size 252,000
Annual Interest Rate 24%
Duration (months) 4 or 6
Payment Frequency monthly
Security Deposit Requirement 20% of loan amount
Fees
Registration 350
Administration 1% of loan amount
Missed Payment 500/day late + 2% of capital due

CrÈdit Catholic Relief Services (CCRS)
Catholic Relief Services Credit
The CCRS program is designed to be complementary to the TPCF program by increasing the maximum loan amount available to existing TPCF borrowers. Capital is provided by Catholic Relief Services, an American non-governmental organization. Details of the program are outlined below:*
All borrowers
Maximum Loan Size unlimited but subject to need
Annual Interest Rate 24%
Duration (months) 6 or 8
Payment Frequency monthly
Security Deposit Requirement 20% of loan amount
Fees
Registration 350
Administration 1% of loan amount
Missed Payment 500/day late + 2% of capital due

CrÈdit Planète Finance
Planète Finance Credit
Planète Finance loans are meant to bridge the gap between micro-credit and loans from conventional banks. They are designed for individuals with established businesses who need additional capital to expand their activity. Planète Finance loans were created in 2007 and are funded by an international organization Planète Finance. Details of the program are outlined below:*
First time borrower Second time borrower
Maximum Loan Size 1,000,000 2,000,000
Annual Interest Rate 12% 12%
Duration (months) 12 18
Payment Frequency monthly monthly
Security Deposit Requirement none, existing physical capital is collateral none, existing physical capital is collateral
Fees
Registration 5000
Administration 1% of loan amount
Missed Payment 3000/day
Registration -
Administration 1% of loan amount
Missed Payment 3000/day

CrÈdit Warrantage
Warrantage Credit
CrÈdit Warrantage was created in 2008 as a response to increasing food prices. It is meant to relax supply restrictions by giving livestock owners and farmers an immediate boost to their capital with no money down. The absence of a required deposit however means Sia N'Son is careful to monitor their CrÈdit Warrantage clients - each is visited at least once every 15 days. Details of the program are outlined below:*
All borrowers
Maximum Loan Size up to 80% of the value of the borrowerís stock
Annual Interest Rate 24%
Duration (months) 4
Payment Frequency once at the end of the loan term
Security Deposit Requirement none, existing physical capital is collateral
Fees
Registration 300
Administration 1% of loan amount
Missed Payment 500/day late

*All monetary quantities are in FCFA (1 USD = 455 FCFA). All quantities are specified per group member.
