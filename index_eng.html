SIA N'SON MICROFINANCE
Welcome to Sia N'Son Microfinance

In the local dialect of Baatonu, Sia N'Son means “for a better tomorrow”. We are a microfinance institution based in Parakou, Benin. Our organisation is the largest in the region, with satellite offices in twelve cities across northern Benin. We are always looking for new partnerships and opportunities in Benin and beyond. We hope you enjoy exploring our website; please do not hesitate to contact us with any questions.
